01 About

Hands-on care, headed toward an M.D.

I'm Riya — a Certified Nursing Assistant and an incoming Biology (pre-med) undergraduate at the University of Illinois Chicago. Caring for patients showed me the human side of medicine before I ever stepped into a lecture hall, and it's why I'm chasing this path with both feet.
